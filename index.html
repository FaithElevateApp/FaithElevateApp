Welcome to Faith Elevate

Faith Elevate is your daily companion for spiritual growth. Our Bible-based challenges are designed to help you build habits of faith, gratitude, prayer, and love—one day at a time.

“Let us run with perseverance the race marked out for us, fixing our eyes on Jesus…”
— Hebrews 12:1-2

What Are Faith Challenges?

Faith Challenges are practical, bite-sized spiritual disciplines you can do every day. Each challenge is rooted in Scripture and designed to help you:

  • Grow closer to God through daily habits
  • Apply biblical truths in real life
  • Experience transformation in your mind, heart, and relationships
  • Build consistency and accountability in your walk with Christ

How to Get Started

  1. Browse the Challenge Library and pick a challenge that fits your season.
  2. Read the Scripture and daily practice for your chosen challenge.
  3. Commit to the challenge for 7, 14, or 21 days.
  4. Track your progress and reflections in the app.
  5. Share your journey with friends or join a group for encouragement.
